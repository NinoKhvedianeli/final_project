About

Our History

Welcome to Georgian Journeys, where your adventure begins. Born from a passion for exploring the enchanting landscapes and rich culture of Georgia, our travel company was founded by a group of avid travelers and locals who wanted to share the hidden gems of their homeland with the world. From the historical streets of Tbilisi to the serene highlands of Tusheti, the wine-rich region of Kakheti, and the alpine beauty of Racha, we offer immersive experiences that connect you with the heart of Georgia. Join us to create unforgettable memories and discover the true essence of this stunning country.

Our Mission

Our mission at Georgian Journeys is to provide authentic and enriching travel experiences that showcase the diverse beauty, culture, and traditions of Georgia. We are committed to sustainable tourism practices that support local communities and preserve the natural and cultural heritage of our destinations. By offering personalized and carefully curated tours, we aim to inspire and delight travelers, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of Georgia’s unique charm.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading travel company in Georgia, known for our dedication to exceptional service, sustainability, and cultural authenticity. We aspire to create a global community of travelers who share a love for exploration and a respect for the environments and cultures they visit. Through continuous innovation and a commitment to excellence, we aim to make Georgian Journeys synonymous with unforgettable travel experiences that leave a positive impact on both our guests and the destinations we cherish.
